About CJCHT

Certified Women's Business Enterprise leading sustainable footwear manufacturing since 1989

Our Story

Established in 1989, CJCHT is a footwear company who always strives for providing not only quality products, but also the best customer service.

Throughout the years, CJCHT has diversified itself into a group company with multiple core competencies: footwear, sustainability, culture and music, and charity.

CJCHT will always commit to being a socially responsible enterprise in our society. This mission has created a very humane company culture which makes CJCHT stands out in the industry.
