Certified Women's Business Enterprise

CJCHT GROUPS LIMITED has met the criteria for certification as a Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) by WEConnect International again in 2023.

WEConnect International's vision is that a world in which women have the same opportunities as men to design and implement business solutions that create wealth and the sustainable prosperity of their communities.CJCHT Groups has met the four criteria inclding ownership, management, control and independence are determined to be at least 51% owned, as well as managed and controlled by one or more women.Cheers to CJCHT's woman and everyone.

曦爵集團於2023年再次通過全球性組織WEConnect International 的認證，獲得女性企業家證書。

WEConnect International 相信無論性別，每個人都擁有相同的機會能夠為所在的社群貢獻己力，創造物質和心靈都富足的生活。曦爵集團在認證的四個面向中:決策、管理、控管及自主營運等方面，超過51%由女性主導。為曦爵的女力喝采，也謝謝每一位曦爵的同仁！
