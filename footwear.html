CJCHT and Footwear

CJCHT is one of the first Taiwanese company expanded into China in 1980s, servicing mostly North America and West European customers. Starting in 2000, CJCHT also entered the domestic China market. As 3 times winner of "Supplier of the Year" for Wal Mart, CJCHT is specializing in seasonal footwear and casual footwear, with production hubs in Shanghai, Wenzhou, and Putian.
