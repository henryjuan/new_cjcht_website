Commitment and Statement Regarding Environmental Position

Date : 2023.03.17

Commitment Statement: CJCHT is firmly committed to maintaining a consistent and principled approach to environmental issues. Our engagement in activities, whether they influence policy, law, or regulation, is always guided by our core values of sustainability, transparency, and scientific integrity. We recognize the critical importance of aligning our actions and partnerships with our overarching environmental goals and global environmental treaties such as the Paris Agreement. CJCHT will continue to actively participate in initiatives and collaborations that advance environmental sustainability, ensuring that our contributions are both positive and impactful for the global community.

As an organization deeply committed to environmental stewardship, CJCHT believes in the power of collaboration and informed action. Our involvement in initiatives like SSBTi.org, Oscar NPO, and resilient supply chain associations is driven by our desire to contribute meaningfully to the global effort to combat climate change and protect our planet. We understand that our actions, both directly and indirectly, influence policy, law, and regulation, and we are dedicated to ensuring that these influences are always positive and in line with our commitment to sustainability. We pledge to avoid any engagement in activities that could undermine environmental science or create conflicting messages about our environmental priorities. Instead, we will focus on advancing solutions that are scientifically sound, globally relevant, and aligned with our commitment to a sustainable future.
