Our Cultural Mission

At CJCHT, we believe that culture and arts are essential to human flourishing and community development.

Through our various initiatives, we aim to:

  • Promote artistic expression and creativity
  • Support emerging artists and musicians
  • Foster cultural exchange and understanding
  • Strengthen community bonds through shared experiences
  • Preserve and celebrate cultural heritage
