A House / Firefly Cafe

A House Reopened

A-House was established as Taiwan's first A Cappella live house cafe in 2010. Throughout the past six years, offered its stage to many international and local A Cappella groups to sing their hearts out; as well as a place for musicians and listeners to share music and performance info with one another.

In 2016, a newly renovated A-House/FIREFLY cafe is introduced to our old and new patrons. With our vision to bring a little bit of Taitung's beautiful nature to Taipei, we hope our performers and listeners will feel as if they are listening to an outdoor concert filled with lush green. It is also the goal of our management team to bring A-House music to all corners of Taiwan. In the middle of the concrete jungle of Taipei, FIREFLY Cafe hopes to provide delicious food and drinks to quench your hunger and thirst. A House wishes to provide music performances to bring happiness and enjoyment for your heart and soul. Whether you are here to eat, to drink, to listen or to perform, we hope to bring surprises to you and put a smile onto your face, just like how you would as if you encountered fireflies in the wild.

【2016年4月1日起，螢火蟲 Firefly Cafe的開啓！ 】

2016年，我們希望能將「A House」這個表演品牌帶到台灣各地，

讓更多音樂表演者能將他們的作品散佈到福爾摩沙的每一角。

而「A House」在台灣的起點，就在「螢火蟲 Firefly Cafe」。

在這個車水馬龍的水泥都市裡，

螢火蟲 Firefly Cafe希望能以新鮮美味的餐點與香醇好喝的飲料，

滿足你味覺與肚子的需求；

A House則希望以各種不同的音樂表演，

滿足你聽覺與心靈的需求。

好奇嗎？快來店裡讓我們告訴你更多我們的夢想！
