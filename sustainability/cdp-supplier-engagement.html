CJCHT Groups Limited Recognized as a CDP 2024 Supplier Engagement Leader with an ‘A’ Score

CJCHT Groups Limited has been named on the 2024 CDP Supplier Engagement Leaderboard with an outstanding ‘A’ score for our exemplary performance in engaging suppliers and driving climate action across our supply chain.

This achievement highlights CJCHT’s continued leadership in the Textiles & Fabric Goods sector, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to supply chain climate resilience, transparency, and emissions reduction in the Asia region.

We extend our sincere gratitude to SSBTi for their strong professional support and consultancy, and to the dedicated Nanozeo colleagues whose hard work in carbon data management, supply chain footprint tracking, and supplier engagement made this possible. This milestone is a testament to the power of collaboration on our path toward sustainability.

Moving forward, CJCHT will continue to leverage robust carbon footprint management systems, supplier engagement initiatives, and digital tools to achieve even greater environmental impact together with our partners—building a low-carbon, net-zero supply chain for the future.
