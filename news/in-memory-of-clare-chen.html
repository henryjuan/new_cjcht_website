In Memory of Clare Chen

We are announcing with a heavy heart the passing of our beloved Clare Chen, founder and chairperson of the CJCHT Group, the Kehua Foundation, Vocal Asia, and the Shanghai A Cappella Center. She passed away peacefully on May 17, 2022, at the age of 70 surrounded by her caring and loving family.

Being among Taiwan’s leading entrepreneurs, she was able to extend and spread her work and passion throughout the world. She founded the CJCHT Group shoe company in 1980 whilst simultaneously carrying on her two major passions: leprosy service and choral / a cappella music. She always dedicated herself wholeheartedly to both her work and her passions generating tremendous positive outcomes for and within the communities.

Clare started promoting a cappella music in Taiwan in the year 2000 creating platforms for Asian a cappella musicians, amateurs and fans. This eventually resulted in “Vocal Asia” in 2010. Since then, every year, the Vocal Asia Festival has been able to gather people from Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Hong Kong and Macau, China, Singapore, Malaysia, India, as well as Europe and the USA. At the same time Clare Chen facilitated the creation of the world famous VoiceJam Festival in the US, as well as the alliance with the Aarhus Vocal Festival in Denmark. Her contribution to bringing vocal music from all corners of the world to these places of mutual exchange and learning is immense.

She founded the Kehua Foundation in 2017 with a group of friends and music lovers, pursuing the promotion of vocal music and education in Taiwan. In 2019, she brought the Vocal Asia Festival to Japan by collaborating with the government of Kagawa and the Setouchi Triennale, thus creating a whole new experience for a cappella music. In the meantime, she continued to actively support many choirs’ activities, including Fengyou Chorus, the Taipei Male Choir, Vox Nativa, and the Poznan Boys’ Choir.

Clare Chen’s dedication to vocal music has influenced many generations of singers around the world. Her passion, love, as well as her faith, were the driving force in order to create a more harmonious society, in which people are connected through the magic of the human voice.

Clare is now resting in peace with the Lord. We shall carry on her love for music and continue to sing out, remembering her in our hearts.

Clare’s funeral mass will take place on June 4, 2022 at 9:30 (Taiwan time) including a live stream broadcasted for her friends around the world.

化為千風，永存於歌：送別陳鳳文女士

曦爵集團創辦人暨董事長、財團法人科華文教基金會董事長、Vocal Asia理事長及上海阿卡貝拉中心董事長 陳鳳文女士，於2022年5月17日晚間因病離世，享壽70歲。

陳鳳文女士為台灣優秀跨國企業家，所創辦之曦爵集團自1980年起至今為台灣製鞋業表率，事業版圖橫跨全球，並秉持「一個事業，兩個志業」的願景與精神，對痲瘋病公益關懷、合唱與阿卡貝拉音樂推廣兩項志業戮力以赴，全心奉獻，為相關領域帶來難以估量的正面影響。

自2000年起，陳鳳文女士將現代阿卡貝拉風潮引入台灣，並以台灣為基地，串連全亞洲音樂家與愛好者；並於2010年創辦Vocal Asia，連年舉辦亞洲最大規模人聲音樂藝術節Vocal Asia Festival，以一民間企業家之力，將台灣與美國、歐洲、日本、韓國、港澳、中國大陸、新加坡、菲律賓、馬來西亞、印度等地連結為亞洲人聲音樂網絡，成就極為寶貴的亞洲音樂文化交流；同時，她促成了美國VoiceJam Festival之舉辦、策略聯盟丹麥Aarhus Vocal Festival，使得亞洲、歐洲、美國之間的人聲音樂產生影響極為深遠的實質流動。

2017年，她與志同道合的好友們創辦了財團法人科華文教基金會，以「樂計畫」之名，加強推動台灣人聲音樂教育的進行。2019年，她率領Vocal Asia Festival團隊與日本香川縣政府及瀨戶內國際藝術祭攜手合作，讓亞洲阿卡貝拉音樂的推廣達到了全新的高度。同時她對於合唱音樂發展的關心亦不落人後，協助了包括逢友合唱團、拉縴人男聲合唱團、原聲童聲合唱團、波茲南童聲合唱團等優秀合唱團的展演與經營推動。

陳鳳文女士對於音樂志業的奉獻，影響了全球數個世代的阿卡貝拉音樂人以及合唱人；她用她的真心和熱血，加上溫柔的關愛，包覆以經營企業的鋼鐵意志與對天主的信仰，在她的一生中盡心竭力，只為了這個世界能因為歌聲而更加和諧美好，人們能因為歌聲而連結彼此，不再有隔閡。

如今陳鳳文女士已回歸天家，安息主懷，我們將繼承她的志業與心願，持續讓美麗的人聲音樂流轉於人間。她將成為我們的歌，永遠被唱頌著，共鳴著，想念著。

陳鳳文女士的追思彌撒預計於台灣時間6月4日上午9:30於台北聖家堂舉行，由於疫情緣故和方便全球親友參與，除現場參與以外，亦將安排網路直播，詳細資訊將在稍晚公佈。
