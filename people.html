In Memory of Clare Chen

Founder & Chairperson (1952-2022)

We are announcing with a heavy heart the passing of our beloved Clare Chen, founder and chairperson of the CJCHT Group, the Kehua Foundation, Vocal Asia, and the Shanghai A Cappella Center.

She passed away peacefully on May 17, 2022, at the age of 70 surrounded by her caring and loving family. Clare's vision and leadership built CJCHT into the sustainable, socially responsible company it is today.

Her legacy continues through our commitment to excellence, cultural enrichment, and making a positive impact in communities worldwide.
