Sustainability

Not too long after the world passed the millennium, the world “sustainable” entered everyone’s life. Being sustainable became the trend, all industry was seeking solutions of being more “green”. After some time, we realize a “green shoe” was a long way to go. Not giving up our mission of being sustainable, we shifted our focus to shoe boxes. Boxes are made of paper, paper are made of trees, save paper, save trees! With that simple equation, CJCHT started a small sustainable packaging project. Over the year, our little project has grown into a department: NANOZEO, dedicated to full range PDQ & Pallets design and manufacturing throughout the supply chain in China.

CJCHT has always dedicated her sustainability efforts to R&D and supply chain management. Substantial R&D efforts (see https://www.nanozeo.com) by CJCHT have brought ''less material used'' and ''replaced, reduced, and reused (3R)'' into a possible selection by all corporate associated companies. Featured Sustainable products like ZEO®-lite low carbon anti-humidity corrugated boxes, FreshMagic® freshness preservation solutions, and NFRC Plastic Hangers are all available upon requests. A Green Guideline for packaging is also co-issued by CJCHT and TUV Nord for packaging industry.

We provide a full sustainable PDQ and Packaging Sourcing service from design to production. For further detail, please visit : ZEO Official Website
